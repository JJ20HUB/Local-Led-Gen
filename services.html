Tree Care Services

Reliable Tree Work for Homes and Businesses in Knoxville

Every service includes property protection, safe equipment use, and complete debris cleanup.

1) Tree Removal

Safe and efficient removal of dead, diseased, dangerous, or unwanted trees. We assess fall zones, rigging requirements, proximity to structures, and soil conditions. Whether it's a dead oak near your driveway or a split trunk after a storm, our crews handle it with precision and care.

2) Tree Trimming and Pruning

Professional pruning to improve tree health, enhance appearance, and maintain safety. We remove weak branches, thin overgrown canopies, clear power line conflicts, and shape trees for long-term vitality. Every cut follows ISA standards for sustainable growth.

3) Stump Grinding and Removal

Complete stump removal below grade so you can reclaim your yard space. We grind stumps to powder, remove the debris, and leave your landscape ready for landscaping, sod installation, or new plantings. No unsightly remnants or regrowth.

4) Emergency Storm Cleanup and Storm Response

When severe weather strikes, we respond quickly. Fallen limbs, split trunks, trees leaning on homes, and blocked driveways—we prioritize hazards and restore safety. Available for same-day emergency calls during storm seasons.

5) Lot Clearing and Land Preparation

Selective brush and tree clearing for new construction, property preparation, or fire mitigation. We strategically remove unwanted vegetation while preserving healthy trees you want to keep, maximizing your usable land.

6) Cabling and Bracing

For trees with split crotches, weak branches, or storm damage, we install support cables and braces to stabilize growth and extend the tree's life. A cost-effective alternative to removal when the tree has value.
